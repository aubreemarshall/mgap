Structure 11



Structure 11 is located at the southern periphery of the central plaza. there were a series of plaster floors found during excavation and seemed to be lower quality than those found on the mainland (Graham and Pendergast, 1989).



As of 1990, 5 burials have been identified in Structure 11 (Pendergast and Graham, 1990).



One object found with a adult female burial is a plumbate button-face jar, a relatively rare find in Mesoamerica, pictured below. The image was drawn by Louise Belanger (Graham and Pendergast, 1989).
