Marco Gonzalez

This is the map of all of the archaeological structures found thus far at Marco Gonzalez!

As you go through the site, you can look at what artifacts were found at which structures during previous excavations.

We work with several specialists (including ceramicists and lithics specialists) throughout the field season who can (and have) provided valuable information about where artifacts would have originated from. Where available, information about where the artifact came from will be included in the pop up.

After each season, any new findings will be included on the website, so stay tuned to stay up-to-date on our work!
