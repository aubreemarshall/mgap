Structure 26



Structure 26 was heavily looted before excavations. Even so, researchers found that Structures 26, 27, and 28 are made up of thousands of conch shells. Some of the species of conch identified include Queen Conch, West Indian chank, and helmet shells (Simmons and Graham, 2017).
